About Us

Vacheron Constantin is a prestigious Swiss luxury watchmaker renowned for its exquisite craftsmanship, innovation, and timeless elegance. Established in 1755, it is one of the oldest watch manufacturers in the world, celebrated for producing high-end timepieces that blend traditional watchmaking techniques with modern technology. The brand is known for its intricate complications, such as perpetual calendars, tourbillons, and minute repeaters, as well as its dedication to artistry, often incorporating hand-engraving, enameling, and gem-setting into its designs. Vacheron Constantin's watches are symbols of sophistication and exclusivity, appealing to discerning collectors and connoisseurs worldwide.
